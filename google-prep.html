Round 1 · Format
45 min DSA interview + 45 min Googleyness interview. No system design in round 1.
Interviewers score narrated reasoning and how you handle follow-up twists — thinking out loud beats silent correctness. Expect the question to mutate after your first working solution.
A 45-min Googleyness round fits ~5 min intro + 3–4 behavioral questions at 8–10 min each (with follow-up probes) + 5 min for your questions. Prepare depth per story, not breadth.
